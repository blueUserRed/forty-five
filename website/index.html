Forty-Five is a magnificent Wild West card game where players gather cards in the shape of bullets to outgun their adversaries. You can stock up on new cards at various trading posts, embark on thrilling adventures through a diverse landscape on a dynamic map, and even craft your very own decks of cards. Brace yourself for thrilling showdowns that will transport you straight into the heart of the Wild West!
